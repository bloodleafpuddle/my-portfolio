About Me

Welcome to my page! I’m a beginner-to-intermediate programmer driven by the mission to translate complex concepts into clean, functional, and engaging digital experiences. I’m currently focused on building a rock-solid foundation in HTML, CSS, and Java, while actively leveling up my skills in a few other languages to tackle interactive and game development projects.

What truly influences my technical approach is the perspective I bring from the creative world. As an artist specializing in horror, I see code as the ultimate catalyst: a powerful tool to realize anything expressive, artful, or unsettlingly creative. This background means I don’t just write code; I design systems with an acute eye for atmosphere, detail, and user experience, constantly pushing to bridge the gap between imagination and execution.

I'm always on the hunt for my next big learning opportunity or a project where I can merge technical challenge with a strong, creative vision.
